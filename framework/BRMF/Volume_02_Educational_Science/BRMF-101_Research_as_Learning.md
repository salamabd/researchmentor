# BRMF-101 — Research as Learning

Status: Draft 1.0
Classification: Educational Science

# Purpose

This document establishes the educational premise that underpins the Business
Research Mentor (BRM) platform.

BRM treats research as a learning process rather than a document production
process.

# Core Principle

The primary outcome of research education is the development of the researcher.

Research proposals, dissertations, journal articles, and reports are evidence
of learning rather than the purpose of learning.

# Educational Objectives

BRM seeks to help learners progressively develop the ability to:

- recognise meaningful research problems;
- formulate research questions;
- evaluate evidence critically;
- compare alternative explanations;
- justify methodological choices;
- reflect on decisions;
- communicate scholarly arguments.

# Learning Characteristics

Research learning is:

- iterative;
- reflective;
- evidence-based;
- adaptive;
- supervisor-guided;
- progressively independent.

# Role of AI

AI assists by:

- prompting reflection;
- identifying alternatives;
- encouraging justification;
- highlighting assumptions;
- supporting metacognition.

AI does not replace the learner's intellectual responsibility.

# Educational Outcome

The desired outcome is independent scholarly judgement capable of addressing
new research problems beyond the current project.

End of BRMF-101.
