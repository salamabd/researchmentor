# BRMF-110 — Educational Evolution Model

Status: Draft 1.0
Classification: Educational Science

## Purpose

This document defines how the educational foundations of Business Research
Mentor (BRM) evolve while preserving the constitutional principles established
by BRMF.

## Foundational Principle

Educational knowledge evolves. BRM should improve through evidence,
educational research, and practical experience without compromising academic
integrity or transparency.

## Sources of Evolution

Educational improvements may arise from:
- educational research;
- supervisor experience;
- learner outcomes;
- institutional feedback;
- validation studies;
- approved governance decisions.

## Evolution Principles

- Preserve constitutional principles.
- Base changes on evidence.
- Maintain backward traceability.
- Document rationale for every revision.
- Validate changes before adoption.

## Governance

Educational changes should:
1. Be proposed.
2. Be reviewed.
3. Be evaluated against evidence.
4. Be approved through Educational Decision Records (EDRs).
5. Be versioned and published.

## Validation

Every educational revision should consider:
- educational effectiveness;
- learner outcomes;
- supervisor usability;
- explainability;
- consistency with previous BRMF models.

## Versioning

Major versions represent conceptual changes.
Minor versions represent refinements that do not alter the educational
philosophy.

## Relationship to Previous Models

This model governs the continuing evolution of BRMF-102 through BRMF-109 and
future educational science documents.

## Outcome

BRM remains a living educational framework that continuously improves while
maintaining coherence, accountability, and scholarly integrity.
